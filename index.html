Kayaking

A KAYAK is a small, narrow WaterCraft which is propelled by means of a double-bladed paddle.The traditional kayak has a covered deck and one or more cockpits, each seating one paddler.a spray deck that prevents the entry of water from waves or spray.

PowerBoat

A PowerBoat is a boat which is powered by an engine. Some motorboats are fitted with inboard engines, others have an outboard motor installed on the rear, containing the internal combustion engine, the gearbox and the propeller in one portable unit.
